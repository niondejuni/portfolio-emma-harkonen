About Me

I'm Emma, a UI designer from Sweden who has the privilege of calling Barcelona home. I'm passionate about creating user-friendly, visually stunning digital experiences. Inspired by both the simplicity of Sweden and the vibrancy of Barcelona, I bridge aesthetics and functionality in my designs. Beyond the world of pixels and screens, my heart belongs to my dog, who keeps me grounded and reminds me to embrace life's simple joys. Together, we embark on countless adventures, from hiking in the mountains to strolling through new neighbourhoods all over the world. Explore my portfolio to see how I make the digital world more user-friendly and visually appealing. Let's embark on a design adventure together!
